# College of Business

## Overview

The College of Business provides certificate-based professional education focused on leadership, management, entrepreneurship, organizational systems, and workforce development.

Programs are designed for:
- working professionals
- entrepreneurs
- business leaders
- continuing education students

---

# Initial Programs

## HRM-101
Introduction to Human Resource Management

## Leadership Foundations
Organizational leadership and team management.

## Entrepreneurship Systems
Business formation and strategic growth.

## Organizational Strategy
Decision-making and operational planning.
