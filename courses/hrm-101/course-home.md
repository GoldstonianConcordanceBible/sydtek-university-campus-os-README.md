# HRM-101 — Introduction to Human Resource Management

## Course Overview

HRM-101 introduces students to the foundational principles of Human Resource Management.

The course examines:
- recruitment
- employee selection
- compensation
- workforce planning
- organizational development
- strategic HR systems

HRM-101 functions as:
the flagship “golden sample” course for SydTek University.
