# HRM-101 Syllabus

## Course Title
HRM-101 — Introduction to Human Resource Management

---

# Course Description

This course introduces students to the principles, systems, and practices of Human Resource Management in modern organizations.

Topics include:
- HR planning
- recruitment
- employee selection
- compensation
- workforce development
- strategic HR systems

---

# Learning Outcomes

Students will:
- understand HR fundamentals
- analyze recruitment systems
- evaluate hiring processes
- examine compensation structures
- apply workforce planning concepts
