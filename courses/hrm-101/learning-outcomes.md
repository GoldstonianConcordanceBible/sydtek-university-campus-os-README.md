# HRM-101 Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of HRM-101, students will be able to:

1. Explain the role of Human Resource Management in organizations.
2. Analyze recruitment and hiring systems.
3. Evaluate workforce planning strategies.
4. Understand compensation and development systems.
5. Apply HR principles to organizational decision-making.
6. Interpret employee selection processes.
7. Demonstrate understanding of strategic HRM concepts.
