# Institutional Identity

SydTek University is positioned as a digital education institution focused on future-ready learning, AI-native curriculum, theological systems, business education, and K–12 homeschool support through SydTek Scholars.

The institution is organized around four initial divisions:

- College of Business
- College of AI & Technology
- College of Theology
- SydTek Scholars

SydTek University is built to feel like a structured academic ecosystem rather than a simple course storefront.
